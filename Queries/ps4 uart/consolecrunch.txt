Let me start by saying im writing this because a lot of people dont know how to setup UART for their PS4 this TUT is only for those who have a SAC-001 Motherboard.​
​
Tools you would need:​

A set of screw Drivers to open up the PS4​
Soldering station $30-$150 (depends what you get)​
Solder 0.7mm - $7​
Flux - $5 - $15​
Kynair Wire- $7​
Wire cutters - $12​
(Optional) Hot Glue and Glue gun - $15​
Tip cleaner - $10​
ANY Cheap FTDI USB to TTL Serial Adapter​
Micro-USB Cable​
etc​


Step 1:​

After you have your tools buy any cheap FTDI USB to TTL Serial Adapter, Lightning recommends this one.
